About Church Of Our King

  • About COOK

Church of our King exist to serve and worship to Almighty King.Our contanst passion is the exaltation of Supremacy of Jesus Christ. Knowing Christ, Following Christ And Sharing Christ. Church of our King is also passionate in winning souls through prayer, equipping, discipleship and missions with a new mind,new heart,new life for the King.

Pastor Jerome and Jonalene

Jerome and his wife, Jonalene, founded Church of our King, in 2010.


Our Vision

Our vision - The chief end of man is to glorify God and enjoy Him forever. It is our passion TO KNOW, TO FOLLOW, and TO SHARE JESUS in season & out of season.


Our Mission

Our mission is to fulfill the great commission in taking part in church planting, and international missions, maintaining the sanctity of the word in the life & ministry of the church undergird by passionate prayer.


Our Core Values

We are organized soley for spiritual and practical purposes, We serve to minister in view of Matthew 28:18-20.


Statement Of Faith

We believed in the unity holy trinity, God the Father, God the Son (Jesus Christ) & God the Holy Spirit.
