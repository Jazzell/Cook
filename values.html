Our Core Values

C.O.OK Identity and Purpose

We are organized soley for spiritual and practical purposes, We serve to minister in view of Matthew 28:18-20.

COOK is part of the global effort to reach the least, the last and the lost under"IPHC" with its Philippines counterpart "PPHC" and we uphold the same faith & convictions with regards to the importance of Prayer, Preaching The Uncompromised Word of God, Church planting and world missions.

We are an intentional church and God's agent of transformation driven by a vision.

C.O.O.K Values and Emphasis (Who we are)

Church of our King "LIFTS" Up What is right and what is true.

L.I.F.T.S - Love, Integrity, Faith, Truth

If we don't demonstrate love, hold to integrity, exhibit faith, and embrace truth, then we should not exist as church.

We value the bible as the inspired word of God, as our sole guide for faith, ministry, life & truth.

We value spiritual disciplines, in personal, congregational worship & devotion.

We values the necessity of common edification, the standard of biblical leadership, submission to the Lordship of Jesus Christ and accountability with each other.

We value the spreading of pure & sincere gospel in any acceptable manner.

We vaue the promotion of constant Christ-honoring relationship.

What we do ? C.O.O.K set 3 priorites in all things that we do

We glorify God through public & private worship

We grow in our faith by fellowship & equipping

We give ourselves away in service, mercy, evangelism, missions, and stewarship.

We support the vision of the church by prayer and sow seeds to the vision.

C.O.O.K mindset of Ministry (approach to Ministry)

Church of our King's primary method of making mature and equipped learners(Knowing) Followers, that shares the gospel (K > F > S).

T.E.A.M.S - Truth, Equipping, Accountability, Missions, Supplication

C.O.O.K primary outreach area

Residencial, Relational, Professional, Social

C.O.O.K Ministry Emphasis- "Fruit first and not the gift"

Fruit speaks of our maturity, gifts speaks of our ministry.

There are many gifted people in ministry but not mature - We want maturity before ability.

Personal Outreach - Each One - Reach One (E1-R1) - INFUSION

C.O.O.K Declaration

C.O.O.K is a praying church, worshipping church, loving church, therefore is a safe home where the people of God are equipped with an understanding of God's design & plans for our lives.
