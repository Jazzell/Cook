Praise and Worship Ministry

The Praise & Worship ministry is a team of believers that are called not only to worship the Lord but to facilitate the worship experience, and carry the burden of the worship life of Church of our King.


Praise and worship ministry is called to unite people at the church services in worshipping God. It has to focus their arrention on Him by the means of music and words, talents and gifts, to praise God and to worship Him, to reflect God (His actions, thoughts, will, desires, feelings, character), being in harmony with prayer, ministry of the word and revealing the character of events in the service.

True worship, in other words, is defined by the priority we place on who God is in our lives and where God is on our list of priorities.

Time : 6:30PM to 9:30PM

Ministry Purpose

The Praise & Worship team exists to minister to the Lord through praise and worship, to encourage and lead the congregation into the presence of the Lord, and to facilitate and create an atmosphere where the presence of the Lord can dwell. Our goal is to lead people into the manifest presence of the Lord.

Tasks

  • Preparing disciples and comprehensive assistance in discovering of their potential
  • Laying down spiritual foundation (staying together in prayer and in the Word) and building relationships between ministers (fellowship, helping to fulfill the needs of each other etc.
  • Growing Spiritually and Professionally (both individually and also joint trainings in music disciplines, organizing and particpating in master-classes)
  • Technical provision for the ministry (building team of sound engineers and sound operators, operability assurance of the equipment, hardware for the services and rehearsals)

Why participate in this ministry?

If you have a heart to see others commune with the Lord in worship, if you have a burden for the worship life of Maranatha, if you are a worshipper and you are called to serve in the area of praise and worship, and if you can sing, then the Praise & Worship ministry is for you!
