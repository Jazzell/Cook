KIDS MINISTRY

Reaching and teaching the next generation

Knowing that reaching and teaching the next generation is critical for the continuation of the church of Jesus Christ, Church of our King takes this area of its ministry very seriously.Sunday morning Childrenâ€™s Church is dismissed from the Sunday Morning Worship Service where they meet to have a church service specially suited to their needs. Our church offers a kids devotional booklet to help children develop a habit of daily devotions at a young age.

Each summer there are a couple of highlights in our children's ministry. One is Vacation Bible School for ages four years through sixth grade. The other is summer camp for the upper elementary grades (fourth through sixth). Parents won't have to coax their child to be a part of these exciting, but spiritually profitable activities.

Kids of our King Sunday School

Our children's church ministry encourages children to serve the Lord with missionary and Bible character stories. The children sing vibrant God honoring children's songs that teach them about the gospel. The program is available for children ages four through fourth grade. Children are dismissed during the morning worship service in order to learn about God on their own level.
